# Capstone Project Final Defense Evaluation Criteria

## Technical Evaluation (IT Panelists - 80%)

Each IT panelist evaluates the following criteria:

| Criteria | Description | Weight |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Problem Definition & Objectives | Clarity of the problem, relevance to IT field, and alignment of objectives with the developed system | 10% |
| System Implementation & Functionality | Completeness of system features, system integration, reliability, and usability | 20% |
| Innovation / Technical Contribution | Novelty of the solution, application of appropriate technologies, and contribution to solving the identified problem | 10% |
| Documentation Quality | Organization, completeness, and clarity of the technical documentation | 15% |
| Presentation & Demonstration | Clarity of presentation, effectiveness of system demonstration, and ability to explain technical aspects | 10% |
| Response to Questions (Technical Defense) | Ability to justify design decisions, explain technical details, and defend the system | 15% |

## Research & Statistical Evaluation (Statistician - 20%)

| Criteria | Description | Weight |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Research Design & Methodology | Appropriateness of research design, sampling, and data collection methods | 5% |
| Statistical Analysis | Correct use of statistical tools, data processing, and analysis techniques | 7% |
| Interpretation of Results | Accuracy in interpreting statistical results and linking them to research objectives | 5% |
| Validity & Reliability of Data | Proper validation, reliability testing, and credibility of collected data | 3% |

## Individual Performance Evaluation Criteria

Each panelist, including the statistician, evaluates each student individually during the defense.

### Individual Evaluation Rubric (100%)

| Criteria | Description | Weight |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Knowledge of the Project | Demonstrates deep understanding of the project, including objectives, scope, system design, and implementation | 25% |
| Individual Technical Contribution | Clear explanation of the specific modules, components, or tasks handled by the student in the project | 25% |
| Ability to Answer Questions | Accuracy, clarity, and confidence in answering questions from the panel | 20% |
| Problem-Solving & Critical Thinking | Ability to justify decisions, analyze issues, and propose improvements | 15% |
| Communication & Presentation Skills | Clarity of explanation, organization of ideas, and professional delivery | 10% |
| Professionalism & Preparedness | Confidence, preparedness, proper conduct, and teamwork during the defense | 5% |

## What This Means for Your Current Document

- The paper needs to clearly prove the problem, objectives, methodology, and technical implementation.
- The system itself needs strong functionality, reliability, and a defensible adaptive learning workflow.
- Your group should be ready to explain the AI, XGBoost, FSLSM, research method, and statistical treatment clearly.
- Each member should know their own contribution and also understand the whole project end to end.
